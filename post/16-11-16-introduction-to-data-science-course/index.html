Wed, Nov 16, 2016 Teaching

This is going to be a very interesting challenge since we have 60 first year master students from multiple disciplines: computer science, astronomy, and mathematics. What do they have in common? What can we talk about that they can all benefit from while not being too easy nor too challenging? We’ll see!
